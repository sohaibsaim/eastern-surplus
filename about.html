About EnerSys®

EnerSys® is the global leader in stored energy solutions for industrial applications. We manufacture and distribute reserve power and motive power batteries, battery chargers, power equipment, battery accessories and outdoor equipment enclosure solutions to customers worldwide.

EnerSys® support teams work closely with our US Government/Military customers and Defense OEMs, providing customer support that includes hands-on and in-depth assistance with site visits upon request. Our teams focus on improving equipment operational readiness and ensure that end users receive the most out of our product.

About ABDG

American Business Development Group (ABDG) has been EnerSys®’ exclusive distributor of the Hawker® ARMASAFE™ Plus 6TAGM battery since 2003.

ABDG also serves as EnerSys®’ worldwide support team for the Hawker® ARMASAFE™ Plus 6TAGM battery and the Hawker® MIL PC line of AGM batteries.

We are a full-service business development, sales, support, and marketing consulting firm. ABDG achieves results for our clients while ensuring America’s service men and women are the best equipped and supported military force in the world.

ABDG specializes in the Department of Defense, National Guard, and Homeland Security markets. Our headquarters is located near the Pentagon, the National Guard Bureau, and the Marine Corps Systems Command, and we have offices near the United States Army Combat Capabilities Development Command (CCDC) Ground Vehicle Systems Center (GVSC) at Detroit Arsenal in Warren, Michigan. We’re known for shortening the decision cycle for our clients by developing tailored capture strategies and bottom-to-top-level relationships with Government customers while using our legislative expertise to seek out funding.

ARMASAFE™ Plus batteries are manufactured to strict quality standards in the Warrensburg, Missouri USA plant.
